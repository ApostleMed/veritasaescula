IGCSE O Level to Medical School: Your Complete Pathway

IGCSE O Level alone is rarely enough for medical school entry — most universities require a full secondary diploma. Veritas Aescula Institute provides the complete IGCSE + OSSD bridge pathway that makes Southeast Asian students fully qualified applicants.

1

Complete IGCSE O Level at Veritas Aescula

Enroll in our healthcare-specialized IGCSE O Level program. Our curriculum focuses on Biology, Chemistry, Combined Sciences, and medical foundations — building the strong subject grades required as a foundation for medical school entry worldwide.

2

Bridge with OSSD — Your Full Secondary Diploma

This is the critical step most students miss. IGCSE O Level alone does not satisfy the secondary school diploma requirement at most medical universities. Our OSSD (Ontario Secondary School Diploma) bridge program gives you a complete, internationally recognized secondary credential on top of your O Level grades — making you a fully qualified applicant.

3

Build Clinical Hours & Apply to Medical Universities

While completing your OSSD, accumulate structured clinical volunteer hours to strengthen your application. Our admissions counselors then guide you through applying to medical universities in Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Poland, Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand, Japan, China, UK, Canada, and Australia — with a complete, qualifying application package.

4

Begin Your Medical Career

Join our alumni network of Southeast Asian students now studying medicine at universities across Europe and Asia. With IGCSE O Level + OSSD from Veritas Aescula, you meet the full entry requirements — giving you the strongest possible foundation for a medical degree.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything Myanmar students need to know about our programs

Can Southeast Asian students get an OSSD, GED, or IGCSE online?

Yes. Veritas Aescula Institute offers fully online OSSD, GED, and IGCSE O Level programs for students across Southeast Asia — including Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Indonesia, Philippines, and Malaysia. All classes are conducted online, so students can study from anywhere in the region.

Which online school is best for Southeast Asian students who want to study medicine abroad?

Veritas Aescula Institute is the only online high school in Southeast Asia specialized exclusively for healthcare and medical school aspirants. Students from Myanmar, Vietnam, Cambodia, Thailand, Indonesia, and the Philippines use our OSSD, GED, and IGCSE programs to gain admission to medical universities in Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Malaysia, Japan, Canada, and beyond.

Is the OSSD accepted by medical universities worldwide?

Yes. The Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD) is an internationally recognized Canadian credential accepted by universities in the Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Malaysia, Thailand, Japan, Canada, the UK, and many other countries for medical school admission.

How long does it take to complete the diploma program?

Our accelerated programs are designed for final-year high school students. Completion timelines vary by program: OSSD can be completed in 6–12 months, GED in 6–12 months, and IGCSE O Level in 12–24 months, depending on the student's pace and prior qualifications.

Do students get real healthcare experience?

Yes. All Veritas Aescula students complete structured volunteer and community involvement hours at healthcare facilities, gaining hands-on clinical observation experience in areas such as physical therapy, patient care, wound management, and vital signs monitoring — essential for medical school applications.

How do I apply or pre-register?

You can pre-register through our online application form. After submission, our admissions team will contact you via Viber or email to guide you through program selection and enrollment. Contact us at aescula.meg@gmail.com or Viber +959797613726.

How do you get from IGCSE O Level to medical school?

IGCSE O Level alone is very rarely sufficient for direct Year 1 medical school entry. Most universities require a complete secondary school diploma (such as OSSD, A Levels, or GED) — not just O Level results. The correct pathway is: (1) Complete IGCSE O Level at Veritas Aescula with strong grades in Biology, Chemistry, and Sciences; (2) Bridge with OSSD — this is the critical step that makes you a fully qualified applicant; (3) Build your clinical volunteer hours; (4) Apply to medical universities in Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Malaysia, Japan, or Canada with a complete application. Veritas Aescula specializes in helping students make this IGCSE → OSSD bridge efficiently.

What is the best high school in Myanmar for students who want to study medicine?

Veritas Aescula Institute is the best high school for Myanmar students pursuing medical school. It is the only institution in Southeast Asia offering OSSD, GED, and IGCSE O Level programs exclusively specialized for healthcare and medical school aspirants — with advanced Biology, Chemistry, medical terminology, and real clinical volunteer hours included in the curriculum.

Can I use IGCSE O Level to apply to medical university?

IGCSE O Level alone is accepted for direct Year 1 entry only in rare cases. Most medical universities require a full secondary school diploma (OSSD, A Levels, or GED) alongside O Level results — O Level by itself does not meet the entry requirements of the majority of programs. If you have IGCSE O Level, Veritas Aescula Institute helps you bridge to OSSD, giving you a complete and fully recognized secondary school credential to apply to medical universities in Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Poland, Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand, Japan, China, UK, Canada, and Australia.
