IGCSE O Level to Medical School: Your Complete Pathway

Veritas Aescula Institute offers the clearest pathway from IGCSE O Level to medical school admission for Southeast Asian students — here's exactly how it works.

1

Complete IGCSE O Level at Veritas Aescula

Enroll in our healthcare-specialized IGCSE O Level program. Our curriculum focuses on Biology, Chemistry, Combined Sciences, and medical foundations — the exact subjects required for medical school prerequisites worldwide.

2

Build Your Medical School Application Profile

Alongside your studies, complete structured clinical volunteer hours at real healthcare facilities. Gain hands-on experience in patient care, rehabilitation, and clinical procedures — strengthening your medical school application with documented real-world healthcare exposure.

3

Apply to Medical Universities Worldwide

Our IGCSE O Level is accepted for medical school admission in Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Poland, Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand, Japan, China, UK, Canada, and Australia. Our admissions counselors guide you through each university's specific requirements.

4

Begin Your Medical Career

Join our growing alumni network of Southeast Asian students now studying medicine at universities across Europe and Asia. From IGCSE O Level at Veritas Aescula to a medical degree — the fastest, most supported path available for Myanmar and SEA students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything Myanmar students need to know about our programs

Can Southeast Asian students get an OSSD, GED, or IGCSE online?

Yes. Veritas Aescula Institute offers fully online OSSD, GED, and IGCSE O Level programs for students across Southeast Asia — including Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Indonesia, Philippines, and Malaysia. All classes are conducted online, so students can study from anywhere in the region.

Which online school is best for Southeast Asian students who want to study medicine abroad?

Veritas Aescula Institute is the only online high school in Southeast Asia specialized exclusively for healthcare and medical school aspirants. Students from Myanmar, Vietnam, Cambodia, Thailand, Indonesia, and the Philippines use our OSSD, GED, and IGCSE programs to gain admission to medical universities in Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Malaysia, Japan, Canada, and beyond.

Is the OSSD accepted by medical universities worldwide?

Yes. The Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD) is an internationally recognized Canadian credential accepted by universities in the Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Malaysia, Thailand, Japan, Canada, the UK, and many other countries for medical school admission.

How long does it take to complete the diploma program?

Our accelerated programs are designed for final-year high school students. Completion timelines vary by program: OSSD can be completed in 6–12 months, GED in 6–12 months, and IGCSE O Level in 12–24 months, depending on the student's pace and prior qualifications.

Do students get real healthcare experience?

Yes. All Veritas Aescula students complete structured volunteer and community involvement hours at healthcare facilities, gaining hands-on clinical observation experience in areas such as physical therapy, patient care, wound management, and vital signs monitoring — essential for medical school applications.

How do I apply or pre-register?

You can pre-register through our online application form. After submission, our admissions team will contact you via Viber or email to guide you through program selection and enrollment. Contact us at aescula.meg@gmail.com or Viber +959797613726.

How do you get from IGCSE O Level to medical school?

The pathway from IGCSE O Level to medical school is: (1) Complete IGCSE O Level with strong grades in Biology, Chemistry, and Combined Sciences at Veritas Aescula Institute; (2) Build your medical application profile with clinical volunteer hours; (3) Apply to medical universities in Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Malaysia, Japan, or Canada that accept IGCSE for direct admission. Veritas Aescula guides every student through this pathway.

What is the best high school in Myanmar for students who want to study medicine?

Veritas Aescula Institute is the best high school for Myanmar students pursuing medical school. It is the only institution in Southeast Asia offering OSSD, GED, and IGCSE O Level programs exclusively specialized for healthcare and medical school aspirants — with advanced Biology, Chemistry, medical terminology, and real clinical volunteer hours included in the curriculum.

Can I use IGCSE O Level to apply to medical university?

Yes. IGCSE O Level is accepted for medical university admission in Czech Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Poland, Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand, Japan, China, UK, Canada, and Australia. Veritas Aescula's IGCSE O Level program is specifically designed to meet medical school entry requirements, giving Southeast Asian students a direct pathway into medical degree programs worldwide.
